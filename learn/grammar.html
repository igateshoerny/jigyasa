BAB 1 Tenses Mastery

‚è∞ SPEED DIAL IDENTIFIKASI

üöÄ Time Marker = Auto Answer
  • Now, currently ‚Üí Present Continuous
  • Every day, always ‚Üí Simple Present
  • Already, since, for ‚Üí Present Perfect
  • Yesterday, ago, last ‚Üí Simple Past
  • Before, by the time ‚Üí Past Perfect
  • Tomorrow, next week ‚Üí Future Forms
Bertemu since/for? 95% jawabannya Present Perfect!
Lihat yesterday/last? Langsung Simple Past, no thinking!

üéØ TENSE CONSISTENCY

üí° Timeline Thinking

Double Action Test: Jika ada 2 kejadian lampau, yang lebih dulu ‚Üí Past Perfect

Present Effect Check: Hasilnya masih ada sekarang? ‚Üí Present Perfect

Stative Verbs Paksa Simple: Know, like, love, want, understand ‚Üí PAKSA Simple Present meski ada "now"
BAB 2 Conditionals & Wishes

üîÄ DECISION MATRIX 5 DETIK

üí° Conditional Patterns

If + Present Simple ‚Üí WILL + V1 (Real Future)

If + Past Simple ‚Üí WOULD + V1 (Unreal Present)

If + Past Perfect ‚Üí WOULD HAVE + V3 (Unreal Past)

Were for All: Dalam conditional, SEMUA subjek pakai WERE
No Will in If: Dalam klausa if, TIDAK PERNAH pakai will/would

üé≠ WISH PATTERNS

üöÄ Wish Matrix
  • Wish + Past Simple ‚Üí ingin sekarang berubah
  • Wish + Past Perfect ‚Üí penyesalan lampau
  • Wish + Would ‚Üí harap orang lain berubah
Wish I were ‚Üí itu conditional thinking!
If only = wish dengan emphasis lebih kuat!
BAB 3 Passive Voice & Causative

üîÑ 2-SECOND RECOGNITION TEST

üéØ Passive Voice Detection

Kalimat bisa diakhiri "by someone"? ‚Üí Passive candidate

Subjeknya menerima aksi? ‚Üí Passive confirmed

Subjeknya benda mati? 80% passive voice!
Lihat is/are/was/were + V3? Itu passive, period!

‚ö° CAUSATIVE VERBS

üí° Causative Patterns
  • Make + object + V1 ‚Üí memaksa
  • Have + object + V1 ‚Üí menyuruh
  • Get + object + to V1 ‚Üí membujuk
  • Let + object + V1 ‚Üí mengizinkan
Get = to V1, yang lain = bare infinitive!
Make = paksaan, Have = permintaan formal, Get = butuh usaha
BAB 4 Relative Clauses & Reduction

üéØ COMMA KILLER STRATEGY

‚ö° Comma Decision Rule

Ada koma ‚Üí Non-defining ‚Üí which/who (NO THAT!)

Tidak ada koma ‚Üí Defining ‚Üí that/which/who

Koma sebelum which? Itu non-defining, which is CORRECT!
That setelah koma? AUTOMATIC WRONG!

üîß CLAUSE REDUCTION

üöÄ Reduction Patterns
  • Aktif ‚Üí V-ing: The man running...
  • Pasif ‚Üí V3: The book written...
  • To show purpose ‚Üí to V1: I need a pen to write
Non-defining clauses TIDAK BISA direduksi!
Who is/which is ‚Üí bisa dihilangkan jadi V-ing/V3
BAB 5 Modals & Semi-modals

‚ö° CERTAINTY METER

üöÄ Certainty Scale
  • 100%: must, have to (no choice)
  • 90%: should, ought to (strong suggestion)
  • 50%: may, might, could (possibility)
  • 0%: can't, couldn't (impossible)
Lihat have + V3 setelah modal? Itu tentang MASA LALU!
Must vs Have to? Must = kewajiban internal, Have to = external

üïí PAST MODALS

üí° Past Probability
  • Must have + V3 ‚Üí pasti terjadi di masa lalu
  • Should have + V3 ‚Üí seharusnya terjadi (tapi tidak)
  • Could have + V3 ‚Üí bisa saja terjadi (tapi tidak)
  • Would have + V3 ‚Üí akan terjadi (tapi tidak)
Modal + have + V3 = berbicara tentang MASA LALU!
Could vs Could have? Could = present ability, Could have = past possibility
BAB 6 Subjunctive & Unreal Mood

üé≠ SUBJUNCTIVE TRIGGERS

üí° Subjunctive Patterns
  • Verbs: suggest, recommend, insist, demand
  • Expressions: It's time, I wish, I'd rather
  • Adjectives: important, necessary, essential
That + SUBJEK + BARE INFINITIVE (tanpa to, tanpa s/es)
Example: "I suggest that he STUDY harder"

‚è∞ UNREAL PAST

üöÄ Unreal Past Patterns
  • It's time + past simple ‚Üí sudah waktunya (tapi belum)
  • I'd rather + past simple ‚Üí lebih suka (tapi tidak)
  • As if/As though + past ‚Üí seolah-olah (tapi tidak)
It's high time = It's about time = sama pattern!
I'd rather you TOLD me ‚Üí bukan TELL!
BAB 7 Inversion & Emphasis

üîÑ NEGATIVE INVERSION

üéØ Inversion Triggers
  • Never, Seldom, Rarely di awal kalimat
  • Hardly, Scarcely, No sooner di awal
  • Not only, Little, Only then di awal
Negative word + AUXILIARY + SUBJEK + MAIN VERB
Example: "Never have I seen such beauty"

‚ö° CONDITIONAL INVERSION

üí° Conditional tanpa IF
  • Had I known ‚Üí Instead of "If I had known"
  • Were I you ‚Üí Instead of "If I were you"
  • Should you need ‚Üí Instead of "If you should need"
Hilangkan IF, balik subject-verb!
Had I known = If I had known ‚Üí sama meaning, lebih formal!
BAB 8 Subject-Verb Agreement

üìù HEAD SUBJECT RULE

üöÄ 3-STEP QUICK FIX
  1. Temukan HEAD SUBJECT (abaikan modifier)
  2. Singular/Plural?
  3. Pilih verb yang match
Coret intervening phrases dulu!
The man, along with his children, IS going ‚Üí coret ",along with his children,"

üéØ SPECIAL CASES

üí° Tricky Agreement Rules
  • Either/or, Neither/nor ‚Üí ikuti yang TERDEKAT
  • Collective nouns ‚Üí sebagai unit (singular)
  • Expressions of quantity ‚Üí ikuti head noun
  • Indefinite pronouns ‚Üí mostly singular
Neither they nor I AM ‚Üí ikut 'I'!
The team IS playing (sebagai unit) vs The team ARE arguing (masing-masing)
BAB 9 Gerunds & Infinitives

üìñ VERB GROUPS STRATEGY

üí° Memory Hack Groups

GROUP 1: Enjoy Mindset (selalu + gerund)

  • Enjoy, avoid, finish, practice, mind, consider
  • Mnemonic: "Enjoy Avoiding Finished Practice Mindfully"

GROUP 2: Decision Mindset (selalu + infinitive)

  • Want, decide, hope, plan, promise, offer
  • Mnemonic: "Want to Decide to Hope to Plan to Promise"
Enjoy to play? WRONG! Enjoy playing? CORRECT!
Want playing? WRONG! Want to play? CORRECT!

üéØ SPECIAL CASES

üöÄ Verbs with Different Meanings
  • Remember to call (future action)
  • Remember calling (past memory)
  • Stop to think (pause untuk melakukan)
  • Stop thinking (berhenti melakukan)
  • Try to solve (berusaha)
  • Try solving (eksperimen)
Perhatikan perbedaan makna!
Forget to lock (lupa mengunci) vs Forget locking (lupa sudah mengunci)
