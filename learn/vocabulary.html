üéØ Confusing Words

Affect vs Effect

Affect (verb) = mempengaruhi
Effect (noun) = efek, akibat
"The weather affects my mood." (verb)
"The medicine had a good effect." (noun)

Interesting vs Interested

Interesting = menarik
Interested = tertarik
"This book is interesting." (object)
"I am interested in this book." (subject)
Contoh Soal Error Recognition:
"I am very interesting in this subject."
‚ùå interesting (salah)
‚úÖ interested (benar)
