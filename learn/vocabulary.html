🔄 Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms (Sinonim)

Huge → Enormous, gigantic, massive
Assist → Help, aid, support

Antonyms (Antonim)

Polite ↔ Rude, impolite
Generous ↔ Selfish, stingy
Contoh Soal Olimpiade:
"The synonym of 'rapidly' is ______"
✅ quickly (benar)
❌ slowly, hardly, suddenly (salah)
Word Synonyms Antonyms
Ancient old, antique, archaic modern, new, recent

💬 Idioms & Expressions

Break a leg

Meaning: Good luck! (digunakan untuk memberi semangat)
Example: "Break a leg in your competition tomorrow!"

Once in a blue moon

Meaning: Very rarely
Example: "I only see him once in a blue moon."

By the skin of one's teeth

Meaning: Barely, almost not
Example: "He passed the exam by the skin of his teeth."
💡 Tips Olimpiade: Jangan terjemahkan idiom kata per kata!
Pahami makna keseluruhan dari konteks kalimat.

⚡ Phrasal Verbs

Common Phrasal Verbs

Give up

Meaning: Stop trying, quit
Example: "Don't give up on your dreams!"

Deal with

Meaning: Handle, manage
Example: "We need to deal with this problem quickly."

Keep an eye on

Meaning: Watch carefully
Example: "Please keep an eye on my bag while I'm away."
Pattern Soal Olimpiade:
"Choose the correct phrasal verb: We need to ______ the problem before it gets worse."
✅ deal with (benar)
❌ deal of, deal on, deal to (salah)

🔗 Collocations

Verb + Noun Collocations

Correct Incorrect Meaning

make a mistake do a mistake membuat kesalahan
pay attention give attention memperhatikan

Adjective + Noun Collocations

• Heavy rain (bukan strong rain)
• Strong coffee (bukan heavy coffee)
• Fast food (bukan quick food)
🚨 Common Mistake:
❌ "I need to make my homework." (salah)
✅ "I need to do my homework." (benar)

🎯 Confusing Words

Affect vs Effect

Affect (verb) = mempengaruhi
Effect (noun) = efek, akibat
"The weather affects my mood." (verb)
"The medicine had a good effect." (noun)

Interesting vs Interested

Interesting = menarik
Interested = tertarik
"This book is interesting." (object)
"I am interested in this book." (subject)
Contoh Soal Error Recognition:
"I am very interesting in this subject."
❌ interesting (salah)
✅ interested (benar)

🔍 Context Clues Strategies

Strategi Menebak Arti Kata

1. Definition Clue:
"The weather is unpredictable - it cannot be guessed accurately."
→ unpredictable = cannot be guessed
2. Synonym Clue:
"She felt very upset and sad after hearing the news."
→ upset = sad
3. Antonym Clue:
"Unlike his generous brother, he was very selfish."
→ generous ≠ selfish
💡 Tips Reading Comprehension:
• Baca kalimat sebelum dan sesudah kata yang tidak diketahui
• Cari kata penghubung (like, unlike, similarly, however)
• Perhatikan contoh atau penjelasan yang diberikan
